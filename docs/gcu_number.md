### gcu_number



> TITLE
> 
> ------

#### Unit number



> DEFINITION
> 
> ------

###### Unique identifier of the unit.



> DESCRIPTION
> 
> ------

This number is the unique identifier of the unit described. This number should not be duplicated or reassigned to another unit.



> EXAMPLES
> 
> ------

`FIN00001`: A finnish unit's number.
