### geo_datum_delta-x



> TITLE
> 
> ------

#### Parameter Delta X



> DEFINITION
> 
> ------

###### Delta X, Y, Z parameters are WGS-84 X, Y, Z parameters minus the specified datum X, Y, Z in meters.



> DESCRIPTION
> 
> ------

The Delta X, Y, and Z parameters are added to the specified datum X, Y, Z to convert to WGS-84.
